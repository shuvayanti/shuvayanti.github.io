Methods of solving the problem

Cascaded systems - They offer good quality translation in terms of the translated content. However, prosody of the utterance is lost when it is converted to a text and re-synthesized. Thatâ€™s why even though translated content is better than the current direct S2S systems, it fails at achieving a higher MOS score in naturalness.
Direct S2S systems - In terms of translated content they are lacking behind their cascaded systems. But in terms of naturalness they are a better performer. That is because the transformer is able to encode the speaker characteristics which are being reproduced at synthesis.

To be kept in mind

1. Good quality translation in terms of the translated content. BLEU>0.4
2. Good quality synthesis. MOS>4.0/5
3. Speaker similarity
4. Flow of the synthesis in target language
5. Speaking rate.
6. Prosody of the synthesis (applies to prosodical translations)
